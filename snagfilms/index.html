SnagFilms on Roku

SnagFilms is ViewLift's flagship app. Built for the Roku OTT streaming media player, the app works seamlessly with Roku's user interface. Users can download the app and watch free streaming movies, documentaries, and TV shows right from their Roku device or their Roku-enabled smart TV.

SnagFilms on iOS

ViewLift built the SnagFilms iOS app for vastly popular Apple iPhones and Apple iPads. With more and more viewers watching content on mobile devices, SnagFilms wanted to be at the forefront offering their content library to Apple device users. The app works seamlessly between iPhones, iPads, and users' online accounts. ViewLift's marketing team also optimized the app listing and provided additional app marketing support.

SnagFilms for Android

ViewLift built the Android app for SnagFilms to help the company reach the millons of Android devices users. The legions of Android users are also on the forefront of watching streaming content via mobile devices, and SnagFilms knew they needed to reach that market. The app works seamlessly with Android phones, tablets, and desktop devices. Additonal marketing support helps SnagFilms to be one of the popular entertaiment apps in Google Play.

SnagFilms for Google TV

To complement and fully utilize the Android market, SnagFilms commissioned ViewLift to develop an app for Google TV. Built off of the Android platform, Google TV has its own unique user interface. ViewLift helped SnagFilms bring its content to thousands of Google TV screens.

SnagFilms for PS3

With more and more households streaming content from OTT devices like game consoles, SnagFilms new they needed to reach Playstation users. ViewLift developed the PS3 app for SnagFilms to help reach the entire Playstation market, particularly those owners who have not jumped onto the PS4 platform.

SnagFilms for PS4

ViewLift's PS4 app was built for the Playstation 4 gaming and entertainment system. An entirely new platform from the PS3, the app is designed to work with the PS4's system and user interface. Having a PS4 app allows SnagFilms to reach the entire market of Playstation owners who may have jumped off the PS3 system and onto the newer PS4 platform.

SnagFilms for Amazon

ViewLift developed the SnagFilms Amazon apps for the Kindle and Fire TV. Amazon, with its own unique market and loyal customers of the Kindle and Fire, was a natural opportunity for SnagFilms. ViewLift developed the Amazon apps so that SnagFilms could reach those viewers.

SnagFilms for Windows 8

ViewLift developed the SnagFilms app for Windows 8. The Windows app market generally has fewer apps compared to iOS or Android. Seeing an opportunity to provide Windows device users with the ability to streaming content, SnagFilms commissioned ViewLift to build this app for Windows 8
