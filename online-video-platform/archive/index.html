ViewLift is a powerful online video platform that manages your content library and distributes your videos to over 21 different device platforms.




Packed with features and services, ViewLift easily manages your content library securely with optimized video streaming and detailed analytics.

Content Management

ViewLift allows you to efficiently handle complex metadata, images, closed captions, geo-restrictions and more by providing updates to your applications in real-time with an easy-to-use interface. Using the ViewLift partner portal, optimize search results for your content to take advantage of organic search engine traffic across Google, Yahoo, and Bing.

Optimized Video Streaming

Streaming video is our business. By creating multiple bit-rate renditions for every piece of content, we optimize the viewing experience regardless of bandwidth constraints to keep video interruptions and loading times to a minimum.

State of the Art Security

Upload, organize and distribute your content in our secure system. ViewLift's built-in content protection utilizes the highest quality encryption technology combined with configurable permissions at the geographic, time-based and user levels to safeguard your content across platforms.

  • Track & monitor user acquisition, retention, return-rate, engagement
  • Perform in-depth marketing analytics
  • Track conversion rates and model long-term user behavior of audience segmented by acquisition source
