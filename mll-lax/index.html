LAX SPORTS NETWORK



Lax Sports Network is an exciting new sports network launching in January 2016. Lacrosse sports fans will get access to live games, exclusive content, and original programming, 24-hours a day!

Operating on an SVOD model, Lax Sports Network will have streaming content available over their website and through apps for Roku, Android, Apple iOS, Sony, Samsung, and Amazon Fire. Not only that, ViewLift will provide website and app store marketing to Lax Sports Network.

Lax Sports Network partnered with ViewLift to develop the website and the streaming content apps. Currently, the website is a billboard, but when launched in January 2016, Lax Sports Network will change the lacross landscape forever!
