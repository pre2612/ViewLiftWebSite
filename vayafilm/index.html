¡Vaya!Film on Roku

ViewLift developed the ¡Vaya!Film Roku app to stream content over the Roku OTT media player and Roku-enabled Smart TVs. With more and more households streaming content online using their OTT and Smart TV devices, SnagFilms wanted their library of Spanish-language movies and shows to be viewable with the latest technology.

¡Vaya!Film for Android

ViewLift built the ¡Vaya!Film app for Android to run on Android mobile phones and Android tablet devices. The app is also built so that viewers can switch between their phones or tablet computers and even their desktop account.

¡Vaya!Film for Google TV

ViewLift built the ¡Vaya!Film Google TV app so that viewers could easily view Spanish-language movies and TV shows on their television. Built to address the unique user interface the Google TV app, users can switch between Android devices to TV seamlessly.
